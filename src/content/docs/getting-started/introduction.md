YourSite is a modern developer platform that gives you everything you need to build, deploy, and scale web applications. It combines a type-safe backend framework with edge deployment and built-in services like authentication, databases, and real-time sync.

## Why YourSite?

- **Ship faster**: Go from idea to production in minutes, not weeks
- **Type-safe by default**: End-to-end TypeScript from your database to your API responses
- **Edge-first**: Deploy to 300+ locations worldwide with zero configuration
- **Batteries included**: Auth, database, file storage, and more — all built in

## Who is YourSite for?

YourSite is designed for developers and teams who want to focus on building their product rather than managing infrastructure. Whether you're a solo founder launching an MVP or an engineering team scaling to millions of users, YourSite grows with you.
